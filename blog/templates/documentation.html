About the page

This page has been written completely in Python, Flask.
Purpose of this project was creation of my personal portfolio website, where I would display my Python and Data Science skills.

Here I would also like to express my gratitude to Corey Schafer, who taught me everything about Flask. Many thanks, Corey. Corey's YouTube.
