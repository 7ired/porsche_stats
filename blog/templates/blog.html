Road to Junior Data Scientist at Porsche AG

Why Porsche? What does it mean to me?

When I was very young, around 4 or 5 years old I could already tell most of the car makes parked outside.

On the walks with my parents or grandma, I would say it out loud - 'Audi', 'Volvo', 'Opel', 'Seat', and so on.

My older cousin had a computer, and whenever we visited grandma, me, my brother and my cousin would play games. We played a lot of racing games, because they were very simple - just steer left or right and brake. And they had cars, which apparently we all liked. I remember, one day we played a game, where all cars were Porsche. Five year old me fell in love in all of them. It was Need For Speed: Porsche Unleashed (2000). For a long time I continued to play the game, and Porsche cars became a dream. I used to have many Hot Wheels, or small plastic Porsche cars. Up to this day, I collect them sometimes.

But why?

I believe that Porsche is no longer just a dream for me anymore. It's the definition of dreaming, the dream itself. It's one of my first dreams I can recall, which stayed with me up until now, almost 20 years later.

One day I sat down and wondered:

What does it take to become a Junior Data Scientist at Porsche?
I opened LinkedIn, and there it was.



I started reading the job description, and found skills associated with the job post.



At first I thought well, that's only some LinkedIn indicators, it doesn't matter. But then - what if it does?
I came to a conclusion, that in order to compete with so many great and qualified junior data scientists who strive for the same position, I have to check in for all of those points! In the end, you have to make sure, that you've done everything you could.
I've decided, that to show Porsche Hiring Team, that I'm a competent candidate, worthy of a job interview invitation, I am going to make a project on each of those items.

When I finish all the projects, I will personally go to Porsche AG in
Stuttgart, Germany to bring them my CV and show them this website.

Follow along on this journey. I will post updates on each of the projects on the Home page.
You will be able to find the projects on the Projects page. Godspeed.
